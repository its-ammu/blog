Notebook is a small, dependency-free Hugo theme that makes a blog feel like a
ruled notebook. The whole page sits on a dot grid, navigation runs down a colored
margin line, and links and titles fill with a highlighter swipe when you hover them.

There are no web fonts and no JavaScript beyond a tiny dark-mode toggle, so pages
load fast and render with your system's native UI font.

## What you get

- A sticky **margin spine nav** with an underline marking the current page
- **Post cards** with summaries, reading time, and tags
- **Dark mode** that remembers your choice
- A **highlighter** accent you can recolor with a single CSS variable

Flip the toggle at the bottom of the nav to see dark mode, and hover a link below
to see the marker effect: [the Hugo docs](https://gohugo.io/documentation/).
